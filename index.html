    History of Education in America

  • 17/18th Century Education
    • • Education Laws in Massachusetts, Why Education Began
    • • Academies/Grammar-schools/Tutors/Apprenticeships (religious based, basic R's, dame sch)
    • • Boys, Girls, and Minorities (Different Subjects, Boys Sent to UK, Literacy Rates)
  • 19th Century Education
    • • Common School Movement (Public & Free, Better Citizens, Post Civil War)
    • • Emergence of Textbooks (Focused More on Morals than Academics)
    • • Public Schools Beyond Grade Schools (High Schools, College Prep, Taxes, Donation of Land)
  • 20th Century Education
    • • Public/Private Schools (Free/Mandatory Public, Had Buses, Rich Opted Out of Public)
    • • Minority Education (Natives, Asians, Hispanics, Disabled; Discrimination Laws)
    • • Post WWI/Cold War (Head Start, Job Corps, School Lunch, Programs etc; STEM)
  • 21st Century Education
    • • Technology Boom (Online Classes/Schools, Internet, PC's, Blackboard, Smartboard, Etutors)
    • • Financial Aid (Merit vs Need, No Fedaid for Intern. Students, Shortage of Gov Funding Now)
    • • Educational Revolution (Personalized Learning/Schedules, 21st Cent Skills, 3 R's > 4 C's)
